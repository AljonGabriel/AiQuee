About

The web quiz app lets users test their knowledge in subjects and grade levels. Customizing their quiz, users navigate timed multiple-choice questions. Instant feedback, including scores and explanations, enhances the educational experience. A comprehensive scope section provides an overview of the chosen subject. With a user-friendly design and dynamic functionality, the app offers an enjoyable way to assess and enhance knowledge.
